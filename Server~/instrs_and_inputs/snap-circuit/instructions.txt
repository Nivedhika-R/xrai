0. Place the transparent board so that the long side is facing you.
1. Place a 5-connector from 1F to 5F horizontally.
2. Place a 4-connector from E8 to B8 vertically.
3. Place a 6-connector form B1 to B6 horizontally.
4. Place the Batteries from 6F to 8F, with the batteries facing you.
5. Place the Music IC (U1) from E1 to E3.
6. Place the Speaker (SP) E4 to E6.
7. Place the PhotoResistor (RP) from D3 to B3.
8. Place the LED (D1) from E6 to E8.
9. Place the Switch (S1) from B6 to B8.
10. Place a 3-connector from D2 to B2.
11. Place a 2-connector from E3 to E4.
12. Place a 2-connector from F1 to E1.
13. Place a 2-connector from F5 to F6.
14. Place a 1-connector on F8.
15. Place a 2-connector from F8 to E8.
16. You're done! Turn on the Switch (S1) and cover the PhotoResistor (RP)!
